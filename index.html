Biography

I received the Ph.D. degree in 2022.10. I has over ten publications, including IEEE TKDE, IEEE TSMC-Systems, IEEE TCYB, IEEE TITS, IEEE TCSS, KBS and ESWA. My current research interests include machine learning, intelligent optimization algorithms, extreme learning machine, and evolutionary computation. I am a reviewer of some international journals, such as IEEE TCYB, IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TITS and IEEE/CAA JAS.
